Who is Mukunandan?

Mukunandan is a student and politician from kc high school. He has dreams of becoming a professional janitor, and hopes that he can achieve his dream before 3201 AD. However, he has even more ambitious dreams, such as becoming a mailman, and potentially delivering for dominos pizzeria, which shows that mukunandan is ready to put in the work for anything.

Abilties of Mukunandan

Flight, invisibility, and terrible humor
